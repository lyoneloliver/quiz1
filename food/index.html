Surabaya's
Traditional Food

Surabaya is not only known as the City of Heroes but also for its diverse culinary treasures. From hearty soups to unique street food, every dish carries the bold flavors of East Java with a touch of local tradition. The food here reflects the warmth and character of the people, making Surabaya a true paradise for food lovers.

Rawon

Beef soup with black broth made from kluwek, served with rice and bean sprouts.

Rujak Cingur

Mix of vegetables, lontong, tofu, tempe, and cow's snout with spicy petis sauce.

Lontong Balap

Lontong with bean sprouts, fried tofu, lentho, and savory broth.

Tahu Tek

Fried tofu, potatoes, and egg topped with peanut-petis sauce and crackers.

Sate Klopo

Grilled meat skewers coated with grated coconut and spices, served with rice cake.

Tahu Campur

A hearty dish combining fried tofu, noodles, lettuce, cassava cakes, and sliced beef in a warm savory broth enriched with petis.
