Food Culture

Surabaya’s food culture is known for its bold and spicy flavors, where sambal (chili paste) is almost always present on the table. The cuisine reflects a blend of influences from Javanese, Madurese, Chinese, and Arab traditions, making it rich and diverse. Portions are usually generous and hearty, matching the lively and outspoken character of the people of Surabaya.

Why You Should Try Them

  • Unique flavors you can’t find elsewhere.
  • Made with fresh, local ingredients.
  • Represents the culture and history of the community.
  • Every dish has a story behind it.
